The importance of user authentication and authorization

Why least privilege is important in operating systems

What I did

This week helped me understand that security is not only about installing tools but also about planning.
I learned that even a simple mistake, such as using weak passwords or giving too many permissions, can cause serious security issues.
This knowledge is important for managing Linux systems safely.
